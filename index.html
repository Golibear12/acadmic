My Academic Story

Pursuing a BSc (Hons) in Physics at Kirori Mal College, University of Delhi, I’ve always been driven by curiosity and a desire to connect logic with creativity. Alongside my major, I am also completing a Minor in Computer Science, which has broadened my understanding of computational thinking and digital problem-solving.


My academic journey has been shaped by success in rigorous entrance exams like JEE and CUET, where I performed strongly in Physics, Chemistry, and Mathematics, securing admission to one of the top colleges of Delhi University.


Over time, I’ve built a solid foundation in core subjects such as:

  • Classical Mechanics
  • Electrodynamics
  • Quantum Physics
  • Linear Algebra & Calculus

What makes my learning approach unique is the blend of theory and application. Whether it's simulating physical phenomena using Python libraries like NumPy, Pandas, and Matplotlib, or designing creative interactive demos with GSAP and JavaScript, I seek to understand and express ideas across disciplines.


In addition to coursework, I actively engage in:

  • Academic Projects exploring physics through computation
  • Webinars and seminars on topics like dark matter, science communication, and emerging tech
  • Self-driven learning through books, online courses, and coding practice


But academics for me is more than just study — it's a form of expression. My involvement in theatre, my passion for music, and love for reading non-fiction and scientific literature give me different lenses to view the world and communicate complex ideas.


This journey isn’t about mastering one path — it’s about discovering how logic, creativity, and curiosity intersect to build something meaningful.
